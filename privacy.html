Read to Unlock

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

Overview

Read to Unlock ("we", "our", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a user.

By using the app, you agree to this policy. If you have questions, contact us at support@readtounlock.org.

Data We Collect

  • Account information: Email address collected during sign-in via Google or Apple. Used to identify your account and sync data across devices.
  • Reading progress: Pages read, daily goals completed, reading streaks, books accessed. Used to power the app's core functionality.
  • Blocked app selections: Which apps you choose to block. Stored locally and on our server to restore your settings.
  • Subscription status: Whether you have an active Pro subscription. Managed via Apple In-App Purchases (StoreKit).

We do not collect your location, contacts, photos, health data, or financial information.

How We Use Your Data

  • To authenticate you and maintain your account
  • To sync your reading progress, goals, and settings
  • To generate quiz questions based on your reading (text excerpts are sent to OpenAI — see below)
  • To validate and manage your Pro subscription
  • To respond to your support requests

We do not sell your personal data. We do not use your data for advertising.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Sign-In: Used for authentication. Governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple Sign-In: Used for authentication. Governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • OpenAI API: Used to generate comprehension quiz questions. Text excerpts from your current reading session may be sent to OpenAI to produce questions. No personal information is included in these requests. Governed by OpenAI's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple StoreKit: Used to process in-app purchases. Apple handles all payment data — we never see your card information.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on our secure cloud server and locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. We use industry-standard security practices to protect data in transit and at rest.

We retain your account data as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your data is removed from our servers.

Screen Time and Family Controls

Read to Unlock uses Apple's Family Controls framework to block selected apps via iOS Screen Time. We do not access or collect any Screen Time data beyond the specific permissions required to block the apps you select. This data is managed locally by iOS.

Children's Privacy

Read to Unlock is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us at support@readtounlock.org.

You can delete your account and associated data directly in the app or by contacting us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Significant changes will be communicated via the app or email. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions, concerns, or data requests: support@readtounlock.org
